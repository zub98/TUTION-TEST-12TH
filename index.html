Issue of Debentures - Test Paper

Total Marks: 20 | Time: 1 Hour

Instructions:

  • All questions are compulsory
  • Each question carries 2 marks
  • Total number of questions: 10
  • Time allowed: 1 hour

1. Define debentures and explain their key features. (2 marks)

2. What are the different types of debentures? Explain any two. (2 marks)

3. Explain the term 'Issue of debentures at premium'. (2 marks)

4. What is meant by 'Issue of debentures at discount'? (2 marks)

5. Explain the concept of 'Debentures issued as collateral security'. (2 marks)

6. What are the journal entries for issuing debentures at par? (2 marks)

7. Describe the process of interest payment on debentures. (2 marks)

8. What is 'Writing off discount on issue of debentures'? (2 marks)

9. Explain the term 'Redemption of debentures'. (2 marks)

10. What are the different methods of redemption of debentures? (2 marks)
