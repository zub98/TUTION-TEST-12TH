FOR MAHAK, YASHIKA, BINKLE, KANIKA, ANCHAL
ALAM BHAIYA CLASSES

English Poetry Test Paper

Total Marks: 30 | Time: 1 Hour

Instructions:

  • All questions are compulsory
  • Each question carries 2 marks
  • Total number of questions: 15
  • Time allowed: 1 hour
Section A: The Voice of the Rain (10 Marks)

1. How does the poet describe the journey of rain in "The Voice of the Rain"? (2 marks)

2. What does the rain call itself in the poem? Explain the significance. (2 marks)

3. How does the rain describe its purpose and identity in the poem? (2 marks)

4. How does the rain describe its transformation from vapor to rain in the poem? (2 marks)

5. How does the rain justify its role in the cycle of life? (2 marks)

Section B: Childhood (10 Marks)

6. What does the poet mean by "Where did my childhood go"? (2 marks)

7. How does the poet realize that he has lost his childhood? (2 marks)

8. What does the poet mean when he says "All adults are hypocrites"? (2 marks)

9. What role does rationality play in the loss of childhood according to the poem? (2 marks)

10. How does the poet describe the moment of self-realization? (2 marks)

Section C: Father to Son (10 Marks)

11. What is the central theme of the poem "Father to Son"? (2 marks)

12. How does the father express his frustration in the poem? (2 marks)

13. Explain the significance of the line "I do not understand this child". (2 marks)

14. What does the father mean by "We speak like strangers"? (2 marks)

15. How does the poem reflect the generation gap between father and son? (2 marks)
